Common questions

How do I invite a family member?
Go to the Members page and click the member's name to open their profile. If they don't have an account yet, an Invite to join button will appear in the sidebar — but only if their email address is on file. To add an email, click Edit profile first, then come back and send the invite.
How do I add or change my profile photo?
Open your profile from the top-right menu and click Add photo (or Change photo if one already exists) directly below your photo. Selecting an image uploads it immediately — no save button needed.
My photos aren't loading — what's wrong?
Photos are stored on Cloudflare R2 and are normally very fast. If images appear broken, try a hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac, Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows). If the problem persists, contact support below and include which album or page you're seeing it on.
How do I update my plan or cancel?
Go to Settings → Billing in the admin menu. From there you can view your current plan, update your payment method, or cancel. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period and your data is retained for 30 days.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

OurPeaPod ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your family's privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or use OurPeaPod, we collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Family profile data: Information you choose to add to your family tree, including names, birthdays, photos, and contact details.
  • Usage data: Basic logs of how the app is used, for debugging and improving the service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain your family's pod.
  • To send account-related emails (verification, password resets, invitations).
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To improve the product based on usage patterns.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your family's information for advertising. We do not share your information with third parties except as described below.

Who Can See Your Family's Data

Your pod is private by default. Only members who have been invited and approved by your family's administrator can view your family's information. We do not make any family data publicly accessible.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate OurPeaPod:

  • Railway — application hosting and database storage.
  • SendGrid — transactional email delivery.
  • Stripe — payment processing (for paid plans). We never store full credit card numbers.

Each of these services has their own privacy policy and is contractually obligated to protect your data.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your pod exists. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@ourpeapod.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Children's Privacy

OurPeaPod accounts are for adults (18+). Child profiles may be added to a family tree by an adult administrator, but children do not create their own accounts.

Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, secure password hashing, and CSRF protection. We take reasonable measures to protect your data, though no system is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify active users of significant changes by email. Continued use of OurPeaPod after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@ourpeapod.com.
